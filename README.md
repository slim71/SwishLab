# SwishLab

Turn your phone into a personal basketball coach with SwishLab. Using advanced AI
and motion tracking, SwishLab analyzes your shooting form from video - detecting
key details like release, follow-through, and balance. Get instant visual feedback
and data-driven insights to refine your technique and build muscle memory.
Practice smarter, track your progress, and make every shot count.
